About $CREAM

Written by various Wu-Tang Clan members, including RZA, Raekwon, and Method Man, "CREAM" features a distinctive and catchy beat created by RZA. The track revolves around the group members' struggle to escape poverty and crime through money and wealth. It also touches on themes like street life and the pursuit of financial success.
